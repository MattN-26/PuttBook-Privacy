# PuttBook Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

PuttBook is a mini golf scoring app for recording rounds, players, scores, course details, and local stats.

## Data Stored on Your Device

PuttBook stores app data locally on your device, including:

- Player names
- Round scores
- Course names and course details
- Custom course photos or thumbnails, if you choose to add them
- Local stats, achievements, and progress

This data is used to provide the app’s scoring, history, stats, and course features.

## Photos and Camera

PuttBook may ask for access to your photo library or camera if you choose to add a course image.

Photos are used only for course artwork inside the app. PuttBook does not upload these photos to a server.

## Data Sharing

PuttBook does not currently require an account and does not currently upload your rounds, scores, players, or course photos to a PuttBook server.

## Analytics and Diagnostics

PuttBook does not currently use third-party analytics.

If you install PuttBook through TestFlight or the App Store, Apple may collect crash reports, diagnostics, and usage information according to Apple’s own privacy policies and your device settings.

## Future Features

Future versions may add optional course sharing or cloud features. If that happens, this privacy policy will be updated before those features are released.
